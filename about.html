Meet Katherine Cabrera – Founder & Real Estate Expert

Katherine Cabrera’s journey into real estate wasn’t just a career move—it was a calling. Raised in a family that valued hard work and community, she was always fascinated by the idea of turning houses into homes. After years of experience in sales, negotiations, and customer service, she discovered her true passion: helping people navigate the real estate market with confidence and success.

Discover Our Journey So Far

Katherine’s love for real estate began at a young age when she would visit open houses with her parents, imagining the possibilities each property held. She saw firsthand how a great home could change lives, and that realization never left her. After purchasing her first home, she realized just how complex the buying and selling process could be—and she knew she wanted to help others through it.

Serving the Ottawa Community

Understanding that real estate is more than just transactions—it’s about people, dreams, and new beginnings—Katherine founded Kat’s Daily Realty. Her goal? To create a boutique real estate experience that prioritizes personalized service, market expertise, and seamless transactions. Whether you’re buying, selling, or renting, Kat and her team are committed to making the process smooth, strategic, and stress-free.
