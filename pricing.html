What is a Cubic Yard?

A cubic yard is a unit of volume. It means a space that is: 3 feet long × 3 feet wide × 3 feet high = 27 cubic feet

Imagine a big cube-shaped box that’s the size of a large washing machine — that’s about 1 cubic yard. Our full truck load is equal to 15 cubic yards.
