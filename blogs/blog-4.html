Ottawa’s Hottest Neighborhoods to Buy in 2025

If you’re looking to buy a home in Ottawa in 2024, you’re in luck! The city is home to a diverse range of neighborhoods, each offering unique benefits for homeowners. Whether you’re a first-time buyer, an investor, or looking to upgrade, here are some of the hottest areas to consider:

Westboro

With trendy shops, top-rated schools, and access to the Ottawa River, Westboro is perfect for families and professionals alike. Its vibrant atmosphere and walkability make it a top choice.

Hintonburg

Hintonburg is a charming neighborhood known for its artsy vibe, local cafes, and community events. It’s ideal for young professionals and those who love a lively cultural scene.

Barrhaven

Offering spacious homes, great schools, and a family-friendly environment, Barrhaven is a growing suburb that provides excellent value for buyers looking for a little more space.

Orleans

With a mix of new developments and established communities, Orleans is perfect for those who want suburban living while still being close to downtown Ottawa.

Centretown

For those who want to be in the heart of the city, Centretown offers convenience, walkability, and a variety of housing options.
